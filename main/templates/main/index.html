This is about us

Social Conclave is a one of its kind national conference wherein the delegates from across the nation will gather to discuss about the various social and economic concerns plaguing our country. The main aim of the conference is to find feasible solutions for the problems which are implemented by Team Social Impact. Multiple panel discussions will be held throughout the span of the conference and thus, the delegates would get a helping hand from the renowned panellists. The main objective of Social Conclave is to indulge the delegates in several inspiring discussions and debate sessions, with the hope of changing the face of India for the better. We will also be organising a field visit for the delegates to help them get to the crux of the situation. We hope that the conference will see the birth of India’s next generation of change-makers.

Testimonials

I have been participating in various MUNs and summits since my school days,But Social conclave was something much different and diverse from all those competitions. The whole event was properly managed and hosted. And the informals were just awesome. Something Unique abut the conclave was that it takes the best solutions and try to implement it. The competition is an amalgamation of various aspects such as report writing, group discussions, presentation and solution making along with an actual field visit to places with which the committee is planing to deal with, which actually helps you to know the ground reality of the topic.

Gandharva Shelar

Social Conclave was my very first summit that I attended. I was very nervous and didn't know anything about the concept of a summit. On day one we witnessed many intellectual discussions about various topics. On the second day we were taken on a field visit to our respective area of concern. On the third day we had to submit our final presentation on the the topic that we were working on. When our group had finished the day's proceedings and were sitting in the closing ceremony I realised that I was nervous earlier for no reason. Social Conclave was a experience that I could never forget in the entirety of my life.

Rithvik Shivaprakash

Being a team of freshers last year, taking part in the Social Conclave hosted by the Social Impact was a great end to our first year. Not only the interactive session with experts in fields like Substance Abuse, Urban Sanitation etc. which deals with the problems occuring currently in the society and it also create awareness amongst the students which helped us to gain important knowledge about social issues. The 3 day event was a learning curve in every way with activities like the Panchayat where we discussed the pros and cons of the social issues, the report making session where we documented our research and the presentation where we presented the solutions about overcoming the issue from its root. Social Conclave is a one of a kind event and we are glad to have been a part. So in conclusion, the event was not just about familiarising ourselves with the issues but mainly about how to act on the issues and find solutions. Looking forward to Social Conclave 2020.

Saumya Patni
